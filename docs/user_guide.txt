# User Guide (Surface Level)

Welcome to the YahyaTel v2 Customer Portal! This guide explains how to use the platform's features without getting bogged down in the technical jargon.

## Getting Started

1.  Create an Account: Use the registration page to join. You can choose to be a standard Customer or a Customer Agent (for administrative testing).
2.  Dashboard: Once logged in, your dashboard shows your active services and a snapshot of your account health.

---

## 🔮 AI Churn Prediction (The "Health Check")

What is it? 
Churn is a business term for when a customer stops using a service. Our AI analyzes your usage patterns to tell us how likely you are to stay with YahyaTel.

How to use it:
*   Navigate to the AI Analyzer tab.
*   Fill in details about your plan (tenure, internet type, billing method).
*   Click Predict.
*   The AI will return a percentage. Higher percentages mean the customer is at risk of leaving, allowing our team to offer better deals!

---

## Smart AI Assistant

Found in the bottom-right corner of the screen, the AI Assistant is available 24/7.

*   Ask about Plans: You can ask "What are the mobile plans?" and it will give you accurate prices in OMR.
*   Multilingual: The assistant can translate between English and Arabic seamlessly.
*   Account Help: It can guide you on how to upgrade your internet or add business solutions.

---

## Admin Features (For Staff)

If you are logged in as an Admin or Customer Agent:
*   Monitor Logs: You can see a live feed of all customer interactions with the AI.
*   System Stats: View the total number of users and active subscriptions on the network.
*   Manage Services: Update the available plans and offerings.
